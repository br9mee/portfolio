A little taste of Italy in Oslo

Valentino is a neighbourhood pizzeria and pub in the heart of Oslo. We make our pizzas the classic way — quality dough, fior di latte, and fresh toppings. Whether you're after a quick slice, a full dinner, or just a cold beer with friends, you're welcome here.

Vegan and gluten-free options available on everything.
